Who are we?

Welcome to Flamingo, your one stop shop for events & travel booking!

With Flamingo you can search for your favourite bands or festivals, see where their scheduled events are all over the world, book your event tickets, AND book your flights and accomodation.

At Flamingo we use Songkick's API to help you search for events and return concerts and festivals from all over the world, you can then book your tickets for your chosen event with us through Ticketmaster.

We have integrated Wego's API to allow you to search for and book flights to these events. You can even book your hotel right here with us.

Just click on the Search tab above to get started!
